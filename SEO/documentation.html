What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization

The process of optimizing your website and webpages to get 'free', 'organic' traffic from search engines like Google.

SEO in layman's terms is about getting a website to the top of search engines like Google so that it gets more visitors and may make more money.

Ranking Factors

  1. Make sure that its easy for search engines to understand what your page is about.
  2. Show Google and other search engines that it's 'worthy' of ranking.

Find keywords that are relevant to your niche

  1. Find relevant keywords that people are searching for and see how these search queries fit into your business.

ON-PAGE SEO

involves optimizing content, meta tags and other elements on your own site pages.

You can also refer to it as:

  • Content Marketing
  • Keyword Optimization
  • Images
  • Page Speed

OFF-PAGE SEO

involves any activities that drive awareness and referral traffic to your site from other pages

You can also refer to it as:

  • Link Building
  • Guest Blogging
  • Social Media

Link Building

the process of getting others websites to link to your webpages
