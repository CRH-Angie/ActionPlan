What is SEO?

SEO in layman's terms is about getting a website to the top of search engines like Google so that it gets more visitors and may make more money.

ON-PAGE SEO

involves optimizing content, meta tags and other elements on your own site pages.

Samples:

  • Content Marketing
  • Keyword Optimization
  • Images
  • Page Speed

OFF-PAGE SEO

involves any activities that drive awareness and referral traffic to your site from other pages

Samples:

  • Link-Building
  • Guest Blogging
  • Social Media
